Adaptive Strategies to Climate Change in ASAL Pastoral Communities of Northern Kenya

This research examines the coping mechanisms and adaptation strategies employed by pastoral communities in Kenya's arid and semi-arid lands (ASALs) in response to climate change impacts, combining geospatial analysis with socioeconomic data to identify effective resilience-building approaches.
